Our Mission

Union Capital Boston transforms social capital into opportunity by rewarding community. UCB is an approach to overcome poverty trap for individuals, families, and communities. We are a mobile-based loyalty program for low-income families that provides social and financial service rewards in exchange for community involvement in schools, health centers, and civic programs.

Our Vision

Ongoing community collaboration will create equity for all.
