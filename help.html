Events FAQ

1) How to see upcoming/past events?

Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

2) How to find an event's location?

- Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

- Click on the event you are interested in, this will take you to that event's page which has a map showing its location.

3) How to know the agenda of an event?

- Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

- Click on the event you are interested in, this will take you to that event's page which has the event's agenda written on top.

4) How to know who's going/went to a particular event?

- Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

- Click on the event you are interested in, this will take you to that event's page which shows the event's attendees under the "Who is going" section.

5) How to let others know that you are going to a particular event?

- Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

- Click on the event you are interested in, this will take you to that event's page where you can RSVP to the event by clicking on the "I am going" button located below the list of attendees.

6) How to create or reply to a post?

- Click on "Events" and you will be taken to a page where you can see upcoming and past events.

- Click on the event you are interested in, this will take you to that event's page.

- At the end of the event's page, you can either create a new post or reply to an existing post.

My Diary / Profile FAQ

1) How to add/edit a diary entry?

- Click on "My Diary".

- Choose the event you would like to create an entry for from the list of the events you have attended.

- Below that event you can either create an entry by clicking on "Create Entry" or edit an existing entry by clicking on "Edit Entry".

2) How to upload picture to diary entry?

- Click on "My Diary".

- Choose the event you would like to upload a picture for and click on "Create Entry" or "Edit Entry".

- Click on the camera icon to upload a picture for that entry.

3) How to update my personal/contact information?

1) Click on the dropdown menu on the right top section of the header.

2) Select "Edit Profile" from the list of options.

3) From there, you will be able to edit your Personal and Contact information.

4) Add or edit the information and click on "Save".
